Project 2 - IVIS18

Numbers are in percentage and 0 means there's no data.

Research question

The overall question I was curious in was what factors contribute to happiness in the world so I chose interesting questions which I thought could affect happiness such as religion, family, trust in people etc.

Data transformation

The data had to be transformed into some form that would be easier to analyse. In some questions from WVS I had to choose the answer which yielded the clearest answer such as “unemployed” or “immigration prohibition”. The other answers were different scales of “yes” you could say and therefore I took the “hard” no answers. From the “Happiness” and “Trust in Press” questionnaires I summed up all positive answers to get a somewhat “hard” yes.

Visual mapping

My first thought of visual model to use for 8 variables data was a parallel coordinate system. I think that is a good way to visualize and compare different data points with that many dimensions.

Results

The most interesting results was that I couldn’t see any correlation between life satisfaction and happiness or happiness and religion which I thought would have been correlated with each other.

Learnings

In this project I got to learn D3.js better and I understand now how you are supposed to work with it codewise. I didn’t have that much experience with web programming and with this project I have also been practicing those skills. But the biggest learning outcome for me I think was using the information visualization pipeline in practice.
